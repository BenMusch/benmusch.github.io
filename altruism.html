Donations Log

Millions of people suffer from diseases that are easily and cheaply prevented. I think donating money to the most effective charities is an important and effective solution. I have pledged to give 10% of my income over my lifetime to the most effective charities, though I hope to give more once I work full-time. This TED talk serves as a good introduction to why I pledged.

Looking at evidence of impact, I currently believe bed net distribution and mass deworming are the most effective places to donate to improve the world, though other organizations within the category of global health and animal advocacy may be equally effective.

I keep a log of my donations below so that others can see what causes I find important.
